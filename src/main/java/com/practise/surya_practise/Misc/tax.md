#### LTA=Leave Travel Allowance
domestic travel=flights taken for personal reasons in India
bussiness travel=flights taken for bussiness reasons in India
LTA can be claimed 2 times in 4 years(block year=4 year period set by government) for domestic travel. If we are married then I & spouse can claim 4 times in 4 years, 2 times - each
current block year is 2022-25
LTA is available only under the old regime.
LTA can be claimed by Spouse, Children, Dependent siblings and parents.


Expenses Covered under LTA Exemption 
For Air Travel: Economy class fare of Air India can be claimed as exemption
For Travel by Rail: 1st class A/C fare is eligible for exemption
Other Modes of Transport: If the railways do not exist for the journey route, 1st class A/C bus fare of recognized modes of transport can be claimed as an exemption.
No Recognized modes of Transport:  1st class of the A/C rail for the same distance can be claimed as exemption.
Local conveyance, sightseeing, food and accommodation expenses cannot be claimed as exemption.
